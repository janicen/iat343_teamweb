Final Week

After a long journey of ideation to production, the team has finally concluded a short animation about the life change of our robot character named Otis. Otis is stuck in the 2D world after fighting in the war that broke out 15 years ago between the creatures from the 2D world and 3D world. One day while looking for his compatriots he falls down a hole. When he wakes up, Otis finds himself back in his world, but everything looks different now. Terrified, Otis runs into a random building and a surprise awaits him there.
