Week 3

During the third week of the project, each teammate elaborated on their roles to provide much more polished layout in the character designs and environment designs, as well as making the script to be more easily consumed when developing the animation.

As seen below, the script links to a structure that is easier to comprehend while the images represent the higher quality of the sketches from week 2 with potential colour and shading added.

Script link = script 2

Name: Otis
Character type = robot
Appearance = A robot with a shiny silver-metallic exterior and an antenna on his head to receive signals, as well as two wheels for his feet to help him maneuver around the environment.
Characteristics = Has oil drinking problems, worked at an oil factory

First, this is the scene of when Otis arrives at the factory and finds out that he gets laid off. Here, we explore the colours of what can be a playful factory, yet using the style of a factory.

As for below, we see an environment background of where Otis drinks. After talking through the style of the drawing, the team has decided to incorporate this background and possible to be rendered in 3D of the 3D world.

In the image below, it shows the potential exterior layout of a city in the 3D world that Otis arrives in. Here, it is where the protagonist will stroll around the city and discover its wonders.

The extended version of the vectorized drawing below represents the first scene that Otis first arrives in when he reaches the 3D world. Here, we tried to explore the colour palette of Autumn which uses brown, grey and orange to see if it works with the muddy puddle that Otis will land on.

To end the story, this will be the background scene used, where bright colours are used to stimulate happiness, while maintaining somewhat dark colour as well to tie up with the theme of steam punk.
