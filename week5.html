Week 5

After holding a brainstorming session in the previous week, we have extended our storyboard into a completed version to hopefully capture the idea of the protagonist, Otis, from getting laid off at a oil (beer) factory in the 2D world to finding a new job as a wine connoisseur in the 3D world. Also, in order not to confuse people, we have taken everyday human objects, and translating it into the environment of robots such as when Otis goes to a bar for a drink, he is suppose to be drinking oil, but we have chosen beer mugs as the liquid container to map the similarities.
