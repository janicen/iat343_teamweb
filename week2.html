Week 2

After one week of ideating, the team came back together and met again to see potential directions that we can go with. With further discussion and our level of skills in 3D animation, we decided to pursuit with an idea of a story starting from the 2D world and then into the 3D world. Also, based on the course readings of history in animation and colour, we were hoping to map the concept in the evolution of television technology into an animation such as from the old black and white TVs to colour TVs.

With the idea in mind, the team wondered what type of environment and fictional character can be used. Then we came up with a robot who works at a factory and to add a bit of humor, the robot has oil drinking problem as well. The reason for this character is that we see that animals are used quite often and humans will be hard to model, as well as to rig, so we decided that it will be different to see how a robot behaves in a 2-dimensional world and into the 3-dimensional world. Furthermore, because of our concept in mapping the evolution of television technology, we thought that a robot would represent better.
